Nepal Republic — About This App (English Audio Script)
======================================================

Nepal Republic is an AI-powered citizen platform for Nepal.

It stands between you and Nepal's government. You describe what you need — in plain language or voice — and AI handles the rest.

Here's what it does:

First, AI fills your forms. Whether it's a passport application, a driving license, a land registration, or a hospital referral — you tell us what you need, and AI identifies the right service from over 95 government services and fills out all the required forms for you.

Second, your case gets routed to the right desk. Nepal Republic has mapped 58 government authorities — ministries, departments, municipalities, hospitals, courts, and universities — with 87 active routes. Your request goes directly to the office responsible for it.

Third, every route has a deadline. From 1 hour for ambulance dispatch to 90 days for trademark registration. The system tracks these deadlines, warns when they're approaching, and escalates when authorities miss them.

Fourth, government can respond directly. Authorities receive secure reply links — no login required on their side. They can approve, reject, update the status, or request more information. Every response is logged in your case history.

On the other side of the platform, Nepal Republic tracks public accountability. 109 government promises are scored with letter grades from A to F. AI scans over 40,000 intelligence signals from 80 plus news sources, 17 YouTube channels, and social media. Daily intelligence briefs are published twice a day with audio in both English and Nepali.

The result is one platform where you can get a passport, report a broken road, check if a minister kept a promise, and hear today's accountability brief — all without learning how the bureaucracy works.

Nepal Republic. AI between you and your government.
